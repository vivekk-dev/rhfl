Real Happiness mental shelter home

The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 states that a “disabled person” is one who suffers from a prolonged physical, mental, intellectual or sensory impairment that hinders their full and effective participation in everyday society.
The 2011 census report of the National Statistical Office states that the disabled population in India is 26.8 million (2.21% of the Indian population), which includes 14.9 million males and 11.9 million females. Most of India’s disabled population is economically and socially neglected, with some living without basic necessities.
Since 2021, Real Happiness of Life Foundation (R.H.L.F) is working tirelessly and tirelessly for the upliftment and welfare of the disabled.
Our Rescue Center is one such initiative that regularly caters to countless members of the disabled community. Along with the mentally and physically challenged residents, our center will provide accommodation, sanitary food, medical care, ambulance service and other essential daily facilities. Due to the unfortunate treatment of the disabled in our country, many of them either become homeless or are abandoned by their families.
This disturbing fact makes the task of locating and rescuing persons with disabilities extremely difficult for us. To overcome this obstacle, we at R.H.L.F will work closely with many external sources such as our local police, government. The hospital will work closely with the Hon’ble Court and Social Welfare Department.
Upon arrival of the disabled, the disabled will be fully fed, groomed and medically examined by us. Along with these external sources, we will also release our 24×7 helpline/hotline number through which anyone can inform us about a distressed disabled person in their area.
Our Divyang Community:- We serve people with physical and mental disabilities who are facing different types of disabilities.
Most of our disabled residents suffer from the following disabilities: mental disability: bipolar disorder, Madness, anxiety disorders, eating disorders, Personality Disorders, post Traumatic Stress Disorder, Mental Disorders, a type of mental disorder. Physical Disability: blindness; low vision; Leprosy cured; deaf; of action disability; physically handicapped. rehabilitation treatment At Real Happiness of Life Foundation, we believe that saving someone in need is the first step a rescuer can take towards rebuilding their life.
Keeping this in mind, we will provide various mental and physical rehabilitation treatments for our disabled residents, track and maintain their daily progress. Such therapies include injury prevention, holistic fitness, sustainable therapy, exercise, intensive one-to-one rehabilitation, massage and physiotherapy to develop standardized evidence-based medical modalities to address brain damage.
In extreme cases of emergency, we will take patients to different hospitals in our local area. At Real Happiness of Life Foundation we believe in community living, ensuring that our residents feel at home. To maintain a vibrant and positive environment for our residents, we will celebrate a variety of festivals and special occasions. At Real Happiness of Life Foundation, we look forward to joining you in our journey towards the upliftment, development and equal treatment of India’s disabled community.
