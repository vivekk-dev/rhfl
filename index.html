Real Happiness Of Life Foundation

Real Happiness of Life Foundation is a Non-Governmental Organization established in 2021 in Bhiwandi City of Maharashtra. Our Organization Working for Mental Rehabilitee Center, Old Age Home, Orphanage, Animal Welfare Center, Drugs Rehabilitee Center, Our Organization History is one of great adventures, overcoming huge odds and most important love. We believe on Respect, Care, Help & Smile, Our one of moto is Seeing smile on all needies faces.

Home For Homeless

Since 2021, Real Happiness of Life Foundation (R.H.L.F) is working tirelessly and tirelessly for the upliftment and welfare of the disabled. Our Rescue Center is one such initiative that regularly caters to countless members of the disabled community. Along with the mentally and physically challenged residents, our center will provide accommodation, sanitary food, medical care, ambulance service and other essential daily facilities. Due to the unfortunate treatment of the disabled in our country, many of them either become homeless or are abandoned by their families.
